The Firm

Full-service, by design — not by default.

Venture Law LLP was established to close a gap most firms leave open: the space between a transaction and the dispute it might one day become. We combine deep transactional expertise with a formidable litigation practice, so clients get one firm across the full lifecycle of their legal needs.

Headquartered in Indore with presence across Mumbai, New Delhi and Bangalore, we advise venture-backed startups, institutional lenders, family offices and corporates — and represent them before the Supreme Court of India, the High Courts, the NCLT, DRT and specialised tribunals.
